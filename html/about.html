CONTACTOLOGY WITHOUT BORDERS 2022

The conference aims to promote knowledge of contact lenses and the latest advances in ocular surface among visual health professionals through continuous training.

For 3 days, optometrists, ophthalmologists, and researchers in the ocular surface area and contactology will exchange top-level knowledge and ideas in the scientific, clinical, medical and technical areas related to orthokeratology, contactology in general and the ocular surface.
